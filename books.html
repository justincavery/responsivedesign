Adaptive Web Design

In this book, Aaron Gustafson chronicles the origins of progressive enhancement, its philosophy, mechanisms, and reveals the countless practical ways that you can apply progressive enhancement principles using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. 

By understanding progressive enhancement and how to apply it properly, web practitioners can craft experiences that serve users (rather than browsers), giving them access to content without technological restrictions. 

 Aaron is a wonderful writer and a fascinating individual. Spending a short time with him reinvigorated my desire to build better websites and fortunately this book helps do the same.
